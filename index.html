Rani Laxmi Bhai

Here's a time line of Rani Laxmi Bhai

  • 1828 - Born in Varanasi, India.
  • 1842 - Married Maharaja Gangadhar Rao, becoming the Queen of Jhansi.
  • 1851 - Gave birth to a son, who died in infancy.
  • 1853 - Her husband, the Maharaja, died; she adopted a son, Damodar Rao.
  • 1854 - The British refused to recognize her adopted son's claim to the throne of Jhansi.
  • 1857 - Led the rebellion in Jhansi against the British during the Indian Rebellion of 1857.
  • 1858 - Died in combat on June 18, during the Battle of Gwalior.
